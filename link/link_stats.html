EntLink: stats manager

You can use this tool to transfer stats between accounts, or to reset your stats. You must have fifteen games to clear your stats; you also need fifteen games played on the source account to transfer stats (but target account can be new).

If you don't see your account in the dropdown menu, you have to validate it first.
