EntLink

EntLink links your battle.net account to your forum account so that you can manage your personal ban list, check whether or not you are banned, and more.

  • Validate your account
  • List linked accounts
  • Manage your statistics
  • Are you banned?
  • Manage your bans (on public hosting bots)
  • Upload your own map (to public hosting bots)
  • Host a game (on public hosting bots)
